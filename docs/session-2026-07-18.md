## NS Core sayings mechanic (how it actually works)
On release, charge level `lv = min(1, heldMs / 1250ms)`. Tier is picked by hold time:
- **SHORT** (14 lines) — tap to ~0.75s. Quick icebreakers.
- **LONG** (14 lines) — ~0.75s to ~1.2s. Real credentials (reviews, $1M years, HR-at-scale).
- **MAX** (8 lines) — full charge ~1.25s+. White flash, pink shockwave, pink-glow bubble.
- **SUPER** (4 lines) — hidden, hold **≥3 seconds**. Easter egg; one line invites an email to Nathan.
Everything scales with `lv`: particle count (8→24 dots, 2→10 emojis), burst radius (64→174px), flash (only >0.5), shake, and the release sound's gain/arpeggio. Each pool starts at a random index and rotates so no repeats until a pool cycles. Source of truth: `corelab/nscore_v78.js` (arrays `SHORT/LONG/MAX/SUPER`, `release()` logic).
