The Partner Circle

A partner affiliate program concept for Ritual. A small circle of creators, each with a storefront in their own voice, selling the real Ritual catalog at published subscription prices. Their audiences arrive already trusting the person who invited them.

The economics

How the Circle works

Three sides of one arrangement, printed on every receipt so nothing is hidden.

I

The follower

Receives a private 10% preference on every order, applied automatically with the partner's code. No hunting for coupons, no games at checkout.

II

The partner

Earns 5% of every sale before tax and shipping, including every month a subscription stays active. A kept $100 subscription pays its partner $5 from Ritual, monthly. An annual prepay hands the partner the full year of residuals on day one.

III

The brand

Gains a storefront it never had to design, an audience it never had to buy, and subscription revenue defended by a person, not a discount. Both sides share one ad budget.

Why it compounds

Two audiences, one campaign

The program is built to run dual campaigns: the partner promotes the store to their audience while Ritual's paid media points at the same page.

For acquisition

Every partner storefront is a landing page with built-in trust. Paid traffic converts against a person's recommendation instead of a brand claim, and the partner's organic reach lowers the blended acquisition cost of the whole campaign.

For retention

Because partners earn monthly on kept subscriptions, they are paid to defend retention, answering questions, showing routines, reminding their audience why they stayed. Churn stops being the brand's problem alone.

A note from the author

Why I built this

Ritual already wins on traceability and trust. The Partner Circle extends that trust through people: a follower's first order arrives with a friend's recommendation attached, and the subscription that follows has a human reason to survive. Every page here is a working build, the funnel, the three stores, the carts, the economics, made to show how I think about growth, not just talk about it.
